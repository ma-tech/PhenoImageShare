Filters

Checkbox filtering
Click on a facet to toggle (open or hide) it.
Click on a checkbox to filter your search results.
Filtered results display to the right of the facets panel.
Applied filters appear in the box at the top of facets panel.
Click again on selected checkbox or close the applied filter to unselect it.

Text-based filtering
Click on Anatomy, Gene or Phenotype facets to open or hide it.
Enter ontology term or symbol or freetext in the box provided.
Autosuggest provides you with a list of likely terms.
Click on the GO button after entering term to apply text filter.
Filtered results display to the right of the facets panel.
Delete text from the box provided and click GO to remove text filter.
